# Economy Engineering

Resources enter through harvests, quests, exploration, crafting output, merchant restocks, and event rewards. Sinks include seeds, tools, orchard unlocks, repairs, crafting ingredients, taxes/fees, cosmetics, travel, automation maintenance, and failed-risk recovery. Pricing should combine base value, quality, season, local demand, merchant margin, scarcity, and player reputation. Inflation risk appears when harvest income scales faster than sinks; counter with tiered maintenance, limited contracts, quality gates, and non-currency unlocks. Dynamic demand should publish visible market signals so players can specialize without hidden punishment. Balance validation should simulate day-30/day-100 economies across conservative, average, and optimizer strategies.
